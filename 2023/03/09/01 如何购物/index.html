# 01 如何购物

# 一、去超市

1.supermarket，在美国叫做 grocer store

2.where can I find egg? toilet paper?

3.wheere are the shopping carts? 购物车

4.Do you need a bag? plastic/paper bag 塑料袋 / 纸袋

5.Could you put this in a seperate bag? 单独装袋

6.Does it have a chip? 这个信用卡有没有芯片

7.Sign here please.

大超市

  1. Walmart：东西便宜，一般低收入的阶层比较喜欢

  2. 会员制超市 Costco：可以很便宜地的买到量贩装的产品

  3. Whole Foods ：一般会卖有机食品和健康环保的商品

# 二、去购物

1.outlets：品牌打折店；shopping mall：大的购物中心

2.I'm just looking.

3.Do you have this in + 颜色

4.Do you have this in small? a larger size? What's your size?

尺码特小号小号中号大号特大号
英语extra smallsmallmediumlargeextra large
缩写XSSMLXL

5.Where is the fitting room, please? 5 件 five items.

6.Can you put them on hold for me, please? 能把这些暂时放一下吗？

语音：击穿

我们有时候会念作 put them [pʊtem]，直接是 put 连上 [em]，[em] 前面的 [ð]

是可以省略的，这个现象称为击穿

7. 打折

英文：减价了 30%

They are thirty percent off.

8. 优惠券：coupon [ˈkuː.pɒn]

Can I use these coupons?

9. 会员

Are you a member? 您是会员吗

10. 收据：receipt

Here's your receipt.

11. 退换

I'd like to return + 要退换的东西如 these shoes.
